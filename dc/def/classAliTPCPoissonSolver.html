Provides poisson solver in Cylindrical 3D (TPC geometry)

Strategy based on parameter settings (fStrategy and fMgParameters)provided

  • Cascaded multi grid with S.O.R
  • Geometric MultiGrid
    • Cycles: V, W, Full
    • Relaxation: Jacobi, Weighted-Jacobi, Gauss-Seidel
    • Grid transfer operators: Full, Half
  • Spectral Methods (TODO)
Parameters
matricesVTMatrixD** potential in 3D matrix
matricesChargeTMatrixD** charge density in 3D matrix (side effect)
nRRowInt_t number of nRRow in the r direction of TPC
nZColumnInt_t number of nZColumn in z direction of TPC
phiSliceInt_t number of phiSlice in phi direction of T{C
maxIterationInt_t maximum iteration for relaxation method
symmetryInt_t symmetry or not
Precondition
Charge density distribution in matricesCharge is known and boundary values for matricesV are set
Postcondition
Numerical solution for potential distribution is calculated and stored in each rod at matricesV
