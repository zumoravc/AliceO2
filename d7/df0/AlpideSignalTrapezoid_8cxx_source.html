14 /*
15 
16  This is a simple implementation of the ALPIDE signal time shape
17  via trapezoid. Described by the rise time and the flat-top.
18  Risetime depends on the injected charge linearly: from mMaxRiseTime for
19  charge=0 to 0. for charge>mChargeRise0.
20  Since for the small signals the duration increases, a fraction of the risetime is
21  added to the flat-top duration
22  The time is in nanoseconds
23 
24  */
